TECHNOLOGY

Hummer is powered by a portable wireless routing hardware supporting dual band WIFI, LAN and USB ports, Gigabit RAM and SATA storage (configurable according to requirements) as well as PCI expansion slots. The router is robust enough to support hundreds of simultaneous connections at once. At the same time it can be connected to range extenders to increase the coverage and eliminate dead spots.

On the software side Hummer comes with captive portal support enabling token based/open wifi access. Built in XMPP server allowing both machine to machine and person to person communication. Enterprise commerce, Point of sale and other value added services to enable network operators address the needs of retail, finance, hospitality and logistics sectors.
